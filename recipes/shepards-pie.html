Shepard's Pie Recipe

About Shepard's Pie

Shepherd's Pie is a comforting and hearty dish that combines layers of savory goodness into one satisfying meal. Beginning with a base of seasoned ground lamb or beef cooked with onions, carrots, and peas, the filling is then simmered in a rich gravy that infuses every bite with deep, meaty flavor. Topped generously with creamy mashed potatoes, which are whipped to a velvety smoothness and baked until golden and crisp on the edges, Shepherd's Pie is the epitome of rustic comfort food. Each forkful offers a delightful contrast of textures and flavors, from the softness of the mashed potatoes to the heartiness of the meat filling. Perfect for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends, Shepherd's Pie warms both the body and the soul with its nostalgic appeal and robust taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Tomato Paste
  • Beef Broth
  • Potatoes
  • Cream
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Peel and boil 4-5 medium potatoes until tender.
  2. Mash with butter, salt and pepper.
  3. Saute 1 diced onion in olive oil until soft.
  4. Add 1 pound ground lamb or beef; cook until browned.
  5. Stir in 1 cup peas, 2 tablespoons tomato paste, salt, and pepper.
  6. Preheat oven to 375F (190C).
  7. Spread meat mixture in a baking dish.
  8. Top with mashed potatoes, spreading evenly.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
