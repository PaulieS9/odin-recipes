Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 500-550F or 260-290C).
  2. Prepare your pizza dough by rolling or stretching it out on a floured surface into your desired shape (round, square, etc.).
  3. Transfer the stretched dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
