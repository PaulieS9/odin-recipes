Pizza Recipe

About Pizza

This pizza recipe promises a delightful culinary journey with its simple yet flavorful combination of fresh ingredients and homemade goodness. Beginning with a classic dough, a blend of all-purpose flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and a pinch of salt forms the perfect canvas. After a brief rise, the dough is rolled out and generously adorned with a vibrant tomato sauce infused with garlic and a touch of olive oil. Sprinkled atop is a generous layer of shredded mozzarella cheese, providing the creamy foundation that binds every ingredient together. Customization is key here; whether adorned with traditional pepperoni slices, earthy mushrooms, sweet bell peppers, or piquant onions, each topping enhances the pizza's appeal. Baked in a fiercely hot oven until the crust achieves a golden crispness and the cheese melts into irresistible pools, this pizza embodies the essence of homemade comfort and culinary satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 500-550F or 260-290C).
  2. Prepare your pizza dough by rolling or stretching it out on a floured surface into your desired shape (round, square, etc.).
  3. Transfer the stretched dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
